Adds a sortable column to your product list so you can see how well your Product pages perform.

== Description ==

Add To Cart Rate adds a sortable column to your Products list so you can see how well your product pages get people to add them to cart.

It follow the basic formula of:

Add To Carts / Views = Add To Cart Rate

= Features at a glance =

* Adds the Add To Cart Rate column to your product list
* Records views on your single product pages
* Records when people add to cart on your single product pages
* Excludes add to carts from Shop and Archive pages as we're interested in optimizing the single Product pages
* Reset stats on individual products (coming soon)
* Reset stats system wide (coming soon)
* Exclude logged in visitors (coming soon)

= Compatible with: =

* WooCommerce
* WP eCommerce
* Easy Digital Downloads
* JigoShop (coming soon)

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `add-to-cart-rate/` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Visit the Products list in the backend of WordPress to see the new Add To Cart Rate column
